Jianuo Cao

Hi, I am Jianuo Cao. I am an undergraduate student (Sep. 2023 - Present) majoring in Intelligent Science and Technology at Nanjing University, where I am fortunate to work closely with Prof. Lan-Zhe Guo and Prof. Jieqi Shi. I was also a Visiting Student Researcher at the MSC Lab in UC Berkeley, advised by Prof. Masayoshi Tomizuka.

I maintain the highest academic standing in my department (GPA: 94/100, Rank: 1/151) and am a recipient of the prestigious National Scholarship, the highest undergraduate honor in China. Additionally, I have been recognized with the MCM Finalist Award (Top 2% globally) and the CUMCM National First Prize for my work in mathematical modeling.

My current research interests lie in Robot Learning, Embodied AI, and Whole Body Control. My research goal is to develop generalizable embodied intelligence, enabling robots to seamlessly map natural language instructions to agile, executable actions in open-world environments. I also have a keen interest in exploring AI for Mathematics, specifically in automated formal theorem proving.

I am actively looking for 2026 Summer Research Internships and prospective Ph.D. opportunities (Fall 2027). Please feel free to contact me!
